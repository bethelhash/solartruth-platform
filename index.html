FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything developers and clients need to know before integrating.

The APIs use PVWatts V8 — the same physics model used by the US Department of Energy. On Pro, NASA POWER MERRA-2 satellite irradiance data averaged over 22 years is used. This is the same dataset professional solar engineers use. Results are estimates — actual performance varies by site conditions, installation quality, and equipment.

Most solar tools only model the base 30% ITC. The IRA 2022 created five bonus adders that stack on top — energy community (+10%), domestic content (+10%), low-income community (+10%), affordable housing (+20%), Indian land (+10%). This API models the full stack with every figure cited to its IRS notice or statutory source. A qualifying affordable housing project in a coal closure county with US equipment reaches 60% ITC — a number most tools never show.

Yes. California switched to NEM 3.0 in April 2023, cutting export credits from ~$0.28/kWh to ~$0.08/kWh for new installations. Most APIs still use the old rate — overstating 25-year savings by 30-40%. This API models NEM 3.0 correctly with the avoided-cost export rate, cites CPUC Resolution E-4906, and includes a battery storage recommendation to offset reduced export value.
